How does the Signature Maker work?

The Signature Maker allows you to create a digital signature either by drawing it with your mouse or typing your name and selecting a style. Once you create your signature, you can download it for use in various documents and agreements.

Why use an online signature?

Online signatures, or eSignatures, offer a convenient and efficient way to sign documents without the need for physical paperwork. They are legally binding and widely accepted for various types of agreements.
